FAQ:

Do you have questions about the True Self Worldwide Apps, Library or eShip services?
We've got answers.


Where can I find True Self Worldwide documentation and user guides?

For help setting up your True Self App, please refer to our Guide.

What payment options do you provide?

Our main payment provider is PayPal. If you have problems using a credit card with PayPal then use our alternative credit card payment provider, MyCommerce. We also accept payments via bank wire and invoice. You can find more information about our alternative payment options here or visit our FAQ here.

Do you accept bank wires or invoice payments?

We also accept bank wire / invoice payments directly to our bank account. If you prefer to pay this way, then contact us via email (info@trueselfworldwide.com) with the details you would like printed on your invoice (your address / company name) as well as which True Self App license you would like to purchase and we will send an invoice to you. We’ll email you a link to download the True Self App once the purchase is complete.

Does the price of your App include the books?

Yes. With purchase of the True Self App, you will receive access to all of the digital ebooks in our library for the service term, which is 1 year to start..

Does my plan auto renew?

No, your eShip Service Pak does not auto renew – we don’t auto charge your credit card. However, you can renew your plan at anytime by visiting our website and adding another eShip Servce Pak to your Account

Do I need to renew to continue using the True Self App?

Whether or not you renew your eShip Service Pak, you will still have access to all the ebooks and apps eshipped to your App previously. Renew yoru eShip Service Pak to receive NEW ebooks and updates to our App or Library.

Can I buy just one book?

Yes, all the books in our library are available for individual purchase at the price marked on the website.

Does support include customization or programming help?

Our general support covers installation & general troubleshooting issues via our posted help documentation only. If you have read the help documentation thoroughly and still need help, please Contact Us and we will do our best to help get your True Self App up and running quickly.

Are your books translatable?

Yes, all our themes are translatable using the standard WordPress methods of translation. However, we don’t support right-to-left languages at this time..

Is the True Self App mobile responsive / HTML5?

Yes, the True Self App is mobile responsive and has been built to the latest coding standards. We take your privacy and internet security seriously. Please see our Privacy Policy and Security Statement for more inforamtion.

How often will you release new books?

We don’t set time for release of our future digital content. However, we customarily publish new content frequently. Be sure to sign up for our newsletter to be the first to find out about our newest releases and to stay in touch.

Will you inform me about your new ebook or digital content releases?

Yes sure, we will send you an email notifying you of new digital content. New digital content will also be automatically eShipped to your App upon release.

Do you provide refunds?

Because our digital content offerings are digital products, we do not offer refunds once you’ve downloaded to your computer. If you have any questions regarding True Self Worldwide Apps, our digital content or eShip services, feel free to Contact Us.
